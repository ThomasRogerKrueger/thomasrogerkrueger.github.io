Experience

Sales Recruiting Intern

Walsworth Yearbooks

Summer 2019

Through a career fair at the University of Missouri, I discovered a publishing company called Walsworth Yearbooks. I applied for their sales recruiting internship and was hired to work in their Overland Park, Kansas office. During my time there, I submitted designs for updating their recruiting signage for use at college career fairs. Along with signage, I also researched and put forth ideas on the recruiting items they would hand out at these fairs as well. I was told the company was looking for future sales workers with outgoing and fun personalities, so I tailored my selections to that demographic. Overall, I submitted a tight proposal to revamp their career fair marketing. As a recruiting intern, I was able to sit in on a few of the onboarding and sales training meetings that were done in house at the company for new hires. As a result, I gained insightful knowledge into the mind of the sales force and their techniques for winning deals. I also attended a company wide meeting with the entire sales force. They introduced a new CRM, Salesforce, and I learned much about the importance of managing a pipeline and understanding the day in and day out grind of the sales workers.
